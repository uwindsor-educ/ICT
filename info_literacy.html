Understanding Information Literacy

Information literacy is the set of abilities requiring individuals to recognize when information is needed and have the ability to locate, evaluate, and use effectively the needed information. Why It Matters

Being information literate empowers individuals to navigate the digital landscape, discern credible sources, and make informed decisions. It also helps in academic success and critical thinking.

Key skills include understanding search engines, evaluating sources for credibility, and effectively using and citing information.
